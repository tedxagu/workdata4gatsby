<th>豊増慧介</th>


アメリカ、ベルギー、イギリスで幼少期を過ごす。
成績不良で立教池袋中学から高校への進学を諦め、一年の浪人時代を経て慶應大学環境情報学部へ進学。大学時代は、広告、IT、航空、医療など様々な分野のインターンやプロジェクトに参加。その傍ら、取り組んでいたストリートダンスにて1000人規模のダンスイベントの主催を2度務める。現在はレイヤーズ・コンサルティング所属。


<Row>
 He spent his childhood in the United States, Belgium and the United Kingdom.
Due to poor grades, he gave up going from Rikkyo Ikebukuro junior high school to high school, and after a year of ronin, went on to Keio University Faculty of Environment and Information Studies.
During college, he participated in internships and projects in various fields such as advertising, IT, aviation, and medical care.  On the other hand, he has been hosting a dance event for 1000 people twice in the street dance. Currently belongs to Layers Consulting.
</Row>
