<th>川崎晶平 / 刀鍛冶、エッセイスト</th>

明治大学政経学部卒業
日本刀名匠、全日本刀匠理事を務める
一般企業へ入社後、刀鍛冶 宮入小左衛門行平に入門、現在は作品制作と同時に専門誌への寄稿、講演を行う他、現代刀鑑賞会を開催し日本刀と刀鍛冶のファンを育てる事、また弟子を育て技術の継承にも力を入れている。「テノウチ、ムネノウチ 刀鍛冶として生きること」を出版。


<Row>

He is a swordsmith, essayist, Japanese swordsmith, and director of the All Japan Swordsmith. Graduated from the Faculty of Political Science and Economics, Meiji University. After leaving a general company, he entered the swordsmith Yukihira Miyairi. Currently, he is focusing on creating works, contributing to specialized magazines, giving lectures, and holding contemporary sword appreciation events to foster fans of Japanese swords and swordsmiths and to pass on their skills. Published "Tenouchi, Munenouchi Living as a Swordsmith"
</Row>
