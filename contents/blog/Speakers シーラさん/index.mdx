<th>Sheila Cliffe(シーラ・クリフ)/着物研究家</th>


英国出身。リーズ大学大学院博士課程修了。1985年の来日時に着物に出会い、着物が持つストーリー性やスタイルに魅せられる。着物をファッションとして日常的に親しみ、従来の着物の姿を尊重しながらも、革新的なファッションセンスで着物の可能性を広げ、その魅力を発信。現在十文字学園女子大学教授として教壇に立つ一方、著書出版やメディアへの出演等多方面で活躍中。


<Row>
<th>Sheila Cliffe/Kimono researcher</th>

Sheila Cliffe is from the UK. She completed her doctorate at Leeds University. When she came to Japan in 1985, she encountered kimono and was fascinated by the story and style that kimono embodies. She is familiar with kimono as fashion in everyday life and respects the conventional kimono style, while expanding the possibilities of kimono with innovative fashion sense and an eye for its charm. She is currently active as a professor at Jumonji Gakuen Women's University, but is also involved in many fields including book publishing and appearing in the media.
</Row>
