<th>田中美咲/起業家</th>


1988年、奈良県出身。東日本大震災をきっかけとして、防災に関するソーシャルスタートアップ「防災ガール」を創設。世界のメディアが選ぶ女性起業家として世界一位、国内外にてその他受賞歴多数。2017年より社会課題解決に特化したPR・企画を行う会社morning after cutting my hair, Incを創設、代表取締役。現在大学院大学至善館にて学び直しを行う。



<Row>
<th>Misaki Tanaka / enterpreneur</th>


 Misaki Tanaka was from Nara on 1988. Great east japan earthquake led her to establish startup business called “Bosai Girl”. Took the first place of Women Entrepreneurs Selected by World Media, besides got many awards in and out of Japan.From 2017, established “morning after cutting my hair, inc” which specialized in public relations and planning of social problem. Currently studying at Graduate School of Leadership and Innovation Shizenkan University.
